About Us

We focus on advancing scientific discovery through the integration of Artificial Intelligence and modern Biophysical Techniques. By combining intelligent computational tools with state-of-the-art methods, we aim to accelerate molecular insights and enable more efficient, accurate, and impactful research.

Research Interests

Our lab is interested in the following areas:

1. Protein Structure & Dynamics

Study how proteins are built and how they move, aiming to reveal the molecular principles that drive their functions in living systems.

2. Protein Design

Design and engineer new proteins by combining molecular principles with computational tools to create novel structures and functions.

3. Methodology

We develop and refine experimental and computational methods that advance the precise characterization, analysis, and understanding of proteins.
